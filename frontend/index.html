About GovShield

GovShield is designed to help the Indian government identify and prevent misuse of welfare schemes and corruption in public-funded projects. By introducing a unified database layer for all government schemes and implementing advanced fraud detection, GovShield ensures that benefits reach only eligible recipients and public funds are utilized efficiently.
